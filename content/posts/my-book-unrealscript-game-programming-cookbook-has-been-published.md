My first book, *[UnrealScript Game Programming Cookbook](http://www.packtpub.com/unrealscript-game-programming-cookbook/book#overview)* has been published by Pakt Publishing today. It covers a variety of topics, from creating your own modular camera system, crafting AI, creating intelligent pathfinding for bots, and manipulating weapons.

[A free sample chapter is available as well,](http://www.packtpub.com/sites/default/files/9781849695565_Chapter_03.pdf?utm_source=packtpub&utm_medium=free&utm_campaign=pdf) which covers creating a modular camera system, from beginning to end.

It’s available in a number of formats, including a physical copy, as well as Kindle and .pdf formats. [You can find the book here](http://www.packtpub.com/unrealscript-game-programming-cookbook/book#overview), or also at [Amazon.com here.](https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=1&cad=rja&ved=0CDUQFjAA&url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.amazon.com%2FUnrealScript-Game-Programming-Cookbook-ebook%2Fdp%2FB00BAOC2K6&ei=i9AoUYvHAofs9ASMzIGIAw&usg=AFQjCNGUOCn3Nbx9icBag8ulq18G92DzdA&sig2=568pFLYVavnucFPWGWcuQw&bvm=bv.42768644,d.eWU)
