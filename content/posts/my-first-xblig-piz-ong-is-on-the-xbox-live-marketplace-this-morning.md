So it’s been a long time coming, but my first Xbox Live Indie Game, *Piz-ong,*finally appeared on Xbox Live this morning.

It took me a few attempts to get it through peer review, and it was always for something silly, like forgetting an “if”statement for the trial mode (which  I later found out from [James Silva](http://www.twitter.com/jameszilla) that you don’t even need, as the game automatically prompts for the trial to tend after 7 minutes).

Thank you to everyone who has helped me along the way. XLIG had, and in many cases still does, have a vibrant and helpful community. What began as a project that I started on a whim through the ushering of George Clingerman and Michael Neel, turned into

![](http://davidvoyles.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/xboxboxart.jpg "xboxboxart")

a lesson in both C# and XNA.

I’d love to list everyone on here who has helped, but it is far too extensive. Take a look back in [my development blog](http://davidvoyles.wordpress.com/2012/06/26/dream-build-play-dev-diary-5/) to see in greater detail who has really stood out. Moreover, I’ve written down some [lessons learned along the way](http://davidvoyles.wordpress.com/2012/08/28/some-lessons-from-my-first-game-experience/) too. Also, be sure to check out [DJ Cutman (@DJCutman)’s website](http://www.djcutman.com/), as he did the great soundtrack.  
Is it the greatest game ever made? Perha….hell no. It’s a lesson in C# though, and I’ve learned quite a bit along the way. It’s **extremely** simple, and I wanted to start small before doing it all from scratch with everyone I’ve learned. I’m considering re-doing it and adding a ton of features in the future, as well as writing it all in MonoGame so that it is cross platform, but I’ll need a bit of time to get that one.

You can find Piz-ong on the XBLIG marketplace for 80 MSP ($1). Alternatively, you can purchase it [directly from this link,](http://marketplace.xbox.com/en-US/Product/Piz-ong/66acd000-77fe-1000-9115-d80258550c1e) which will be added to your Xbox Live account when you get home.
