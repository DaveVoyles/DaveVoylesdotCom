Unity is a cross-platform game engine, widely used across the posts in
this blog's archive -- from early indie prototypes through the [[UWP]]
and Xbox One era. Microsoft partnered with Unity as part of the ID@Xbox
program to make porting Unity games to Xbox One straightforward for
independent developers.
