UWP (Universal Windows Platform) is Microsoft's app platform spanning
Windows 10, Windows Phone, and Xbox One from a single codebase. Building
a [[Unity]] game against UWP was one of the more common paths indie
developers used to bring a game to Xbox One during the ID@Xbox program's
early years.
